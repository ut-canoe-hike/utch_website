About UTCH

Tennessee's oldest student-run outdoor adventure club, helping Vols explore the great outdoors.

Who We Are

A Club Built on Adventure

We keep trips welcoming, affordable, and member‑led.

Our Mission

UTCH exists to get students outside—safely, affordably, and often. We build community through shared adventure and make the outdoors accessible to beginners and veterans alike.

Founded in 1968 • Student‑run • Adventure‑first

What We Do

Hiking, backpacking, canoeing, kayaking, camping, climbing, and more—day trips to multi‑day expeditions.

Values

We’re inclusive, safety‑minded, and excited to help you grow your skills. Leaders set clear expectations and keep groups prepared.

How Trips Work

Check the calendar, RSVP, and watch for follow‑up details. Trip leaders share meet‑ups, pacing, and what to bring.

Dues & Gear

Our yearly dues are cheaper than a single trip with other programs, and we provide club gear so you can join with minimal upfront cost.
