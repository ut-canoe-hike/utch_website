About UTCH

UTCH is a student-led outdoor club at the University of Tennessee. We organize group trips and help people get outdoors safely and affordably.

Weekly meeting: 7:00 PM in AMB 27 unless otherwise posted.

What we do

Hiking, canoeing/kayaking, camping, and other outdoor adventures—ranging from beginner-friendly to more challenging trips.

How trips work

Trips are listed on the calendar. Each calendar event includes meet-up details and an RSVP link (with a Trip ID). Officers use RSVPs to finalize the list and coordinate logistics.

Safety

Leaders set expectations for pace, group management, and gear. If you have questions about a trip's difficulty or what to bring, ask before you go.
