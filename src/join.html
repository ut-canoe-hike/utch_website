How to Join

Everything you need to do before signing up for trips.

Membership Steps

  1. Go to the UTK VOLlink club page and click Join: Canoe & Hiking Club on VOLlink.
  2. Complete the required waiver on VOLlink after your join request is accepted.
  3. Pay dues: $35 per semester or $50 per year. You can pay on Venmo at @UT-Canoe-Hiking or in cash to an officer.
  4. Join the GroupMe to get announcements and sign-up updates: Join GroupMe.
  5. Sign up for trips at meetings or by contacting an officer. For limited-capacity trips, active members and regular meeting attendees are prioritized.
