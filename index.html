Get outside with UTCH

We are the University of Tennessee's oldest (and best) student run, student led outdoor club. We offer the widest variety of outdoor adventures of any club on campus, including hiking, backpacking, canoeing, rafting, birdwatching, bouldering, caving, and more. Plus, our yearly dues are cheaper than a single backpacking trip with UTOP.

Check the calendar for upcoming trips, and RSVP to join the roster!
